Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Information

What services do you offer?

We offer a variety of nail services, including dry manicures, dry pedicures, gel polish, nail art, and nail extensions.

Do I need to make an appointment, or do you accept walk-ins?

Appointments are recommended to ensure availability, but we do accept walk-ins when possible.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us or messaging us on social media.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, Seabank, and GCash.

Nail Care & Maintenance

How long do softgel nails last?

They last about more than 3 weeks with proper care.

How can I make my nails last longer?

To prolong your nails, avoid excessive water exposure, wear gloves when cleaning, and use cuticle oil daily.

Do you offer nail repair services?

Yes, we offer nail repairs for broken or damaged nails. Contact us for pricing and availability.

Health & Safety

Are your tools sanitized?

Yes! We follow strict sanitation protocols, disinfecting all tools and equipment after each use.

Do you offer services for people with nail infections or fungus?

Unfortunately, we cannot provide services if you have a nail infection or fungus. We recommend consulting a doctor first.

Pricing & Policies

How much do your services cost?

Prices vary based on the service. You can check our price list on our website, social media, or contact us directly.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 12 hours notice for cancellations or reschedules. Late cancellations may result in a fee.

Do you offer refunds?

We do not offer refunds, but we will gladly fix any issues with your nails within 3 days of your appointment.

Do you offer group bookings or special events?

Yes! We accommodate bridal parties, birthdays, and other group events. Please book in advance.
